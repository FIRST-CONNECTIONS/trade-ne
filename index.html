Who we are

One region. One voice. Real momentum.

Trade NE is the business alliance for the North East of England — built and run by First Connections, the region's networking and growth platform.

Where others speak for a single town or a single sector, we bring the whole region's business community together: the independents on the high street, the start-ups finding their feet, and the established employers anchoring our towns and cities.

We exist to connect, champion and grow North East business — making the case for the region with the people and partners who can move things forward.

We make the case for North East business — to government, to investors and to partners — so the region's contribution is heard and backed.

Flagship events & policy

Putting North East trade on the national stage

Every year Trade NE hosts one to two flagship events built around North East trade — bringing the region's businesses together to celebrate what we do and open new doors. Alongside our events, we shape policy and engage directly with government on behalf of the North East.

In the capital

A London trade event

An annual showcase in London, giving North East trade a voice in Westminster and connecting the region to investment, partners and new opportunities.

On home ground

A North East trade event

A home fixture each year celebrating the businesses, founders and high streets powering our regional economy — and the trade that keeps the North East open for business.

Policy & influence

A voice in government

We shape policy and engage with government and decision-makers on behalf of the North East — making the case for the region's trade, investment and growth.
